Staying Active

Being fit and healthy is very important! If you do not get enough exercise, your body gets very lazy and you can get sick. Being active is easy! Ask your parents or whoever you live with, if they can let you do a sport that you enjoy. Playing sports is a way to make lots of new friends and have a lot of fun while being active and keeping your body healthy. You can also play games by yourself in the rain and sun, inside and outdoors. Being active makes you happier and makes you stronger. This video will show some movements you can do to stay active anywhere.

Indoor Bowling

Ask your parents or guardians, if they have any empty water bottles (you will need 6 or 10) fill the bottles up with one quarter of water, arrange them in a triangle pattern in either the hallway or another open space. Use a soft ball to roll at the bottles to knock them down.

Hide and Seek (Indoor and Outdoor)

Hide and seek is a fun game to play with your friends inside your own home. You’ll need one or two friends and a space set out by your parents where you are allowed to play. Try and make sure there are some hiding spots so that the game is more interesting. After you are all ready, pick which person will be the seeker. The other all go and find hiding spots while the seeker counts down from 20. After they count down, they must go and find the others. Once everyone is found, the first person to be found becomes the seeker and the game start again. .

Freeze

one person is chosen to be 'it' they then choose a song to play, when the song is playing each player moves around in any way they want. when the player who is 'it' pausees the music everyone has to freeze. the last player to freeze has to do 2 star jumps. You can make the game more fun by making the players lay on the floor instead of freezing.

Role Play

This is a fun and exciting activity to play either by yourself or with other people. first think of what or who you want to be for example you could be a king or queen, you could be a cop or a fireman, it's entirely upi to you! Once you have your character start telling a story using your character. For example if you are king you may make one of your friends a knight or you could hold a feast for all your friends. and this game has no end! You can play for days if you want.
