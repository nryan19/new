About

This website aims to educate children, parents and teachers on why it is important to stay active and how to stay active in any situation

The importance of fitness

Being physically fit enables a person to live life with less hindrance of health issues that stem from inactivity. By partaking in at least 30 minutes of physical exercise a day, you can reduce risk of; heart attack, type 2 diabetes, excessive weight gain, high blood cholesterol levels and you can improve your mental state by relieving stress and enhancing your concentration and mood. Exercising is also a great way to get outdoors and meet new people, it can help decrease the risk of depression due to social isolation and provide opportunities to participate in sports as an individual or part of a team.
